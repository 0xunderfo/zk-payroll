How It Works

Four steps. Full privacy.

01

Upload payroll

Enter recipients and amounts via CSV or the form. Data never leaves your browser.

02

Generate proof

Groth16 proof generated client-side via snarkjs. Proves amounts sum to the declared total.

03

Deposit & commit

USDT deposited to escrow. Poseidon hash commitments lock each recipient’s payment amount.

04

Recipients claim

Each recipient claims with their secret salt. The contract verifies the commitment and releases funds.

Privacy

Salaries stay secret

Individual amounts are private inputs to the ZK circuit. Only the total hits the chain — no one can reverse-engineer who earns what.
